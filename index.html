About Me

I am an experienced technical writer and instructional designer with over 15 years of experience, including eight years as a professor guiding multidisciplinary research projects and scaling a leading Digital Humanities graduate program.

Skilled in API documentation, information architecture, and creating user-friendly documentation for complex digital platforms, particularly in the AI domain. Published author of books, articles, and technical presentations, demonstrating strong communication skills across various audiences.
